Omlet Muffins

Ingredients
8 eggs
8 oz. cooked ham, crumbled
1 cup diced red bell pepper
1 cup diced onion
¼ tsp. salt
⅛ tsp. ground black pepper
2 Tbl. water
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease 8 muffin cups or line with paper liners.
  2. Beat eggs together in a large bowl.
  3. Mix ham, bell pepper, onion, salt, black pepper, and water into the beaten eggs.
  4. Pour egg mixture evenly into prepared muffin cups.
  5. Bake in the oven until muffins are set in the middle, 18 to 20 minutes.
