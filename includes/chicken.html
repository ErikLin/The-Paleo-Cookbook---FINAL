Dill & Lemon Baked Salmon in Parchment

Spice Roatsed Chicken

Ingredients
1¼ tsp. salt
¾ tsp. black pepper
¾ tsp. smoked paprika
½ tsp. dried oregano
½ tsp. ground cumin
2 Tbl. olive oil
2 lemons (washed and thinly sliced)
3 lbs bone-in, skinless chicken breasts (4 to 6 piees)
parsley for garnish
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (175°C).
  2. Combine salt, pepper, paprika, oregano, cumin and olive oil until well mixed.
  3. Wash lemons and thinly slice.
  4. Place chicken breasts bone side down in a glass baking dish. Evenly coat with spice mixture and then top with lemon slices.
  5. Bake uncovered for 50 to 55 minutes until chicken breasts reach an internal temperature of 165°F. Remove the chicken breasts from the baking dish to a serving platter. Collect the pan juices and serve alongside the chicken as a sauce. Top chicken with a bit of chopped parley.
